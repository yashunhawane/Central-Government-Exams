SSC Multitasking Exam Pattern

Staff Selection Commission specifies exam pattern for SSC Multi Tasking Staff recruitment. It is conducted through two papers â€“ Paper I and Paper II. Paper I consists of objective type multiple choice questions while Paper II has descriptive questions.

SSC MTS PAPER-1 Exam Pattern

Paper 1 has 4 sections namely General English, General Intelligence and Reasoning, Numerical Aptitude, and General Awareness.The paper will be set in both English and Hindi language except the general English section.The paper comprises of objective type multiple-choice questions.
Subject No. of questions Total Marks Time Allotted
General English 25 25 90 Minutes
General Intelligence and Reasoning 25 25
Numerical Aptitude 25 25
General Awareness 25 25
Total 100 100

SSC MTS PAPER-2 Exam Pattern

Paper II is a descriptive paper and qualifying in nature. It tests the elementary language skills of the candidates. The paper consists of one question on short essay writing/letter writing in English or any language included in the 8th Schedule of the Constitution. Paper II will be of 50 marks
Subject Total Marks Time Allotted
Short Essay/Letter in English or any language included in the 8th Schedule of the Constitution 50 30 minutes
