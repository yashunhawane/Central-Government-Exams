Exam Pattern:

NDA exam pattern is such that it is divided into the written exam and SSB Interview.

NDA written examination is divided into two papers – Mathematics and General Ability.

NDA Exam Pattern for SSB Interview:
The SSB Interview is divided into two stages. Candidates qualifying the Stage 1 are called for the Stage 2 exam. Stage 1 of the SSB Interview comprises Officer Intelligence Rating (OIR) tests and Picture Perception and Description Test (PP&DT). Stage 2 comprises Group Testing Officer Tasks, Psychology Tests and the Conference. This stage carries a total of 900 marks.
