Special Class Railway Apprentice (SCRA) Examination Pattern

Part I : Written Examination carrying a maximum of 600 marks in the subjects as shown below.
Part II : Personality Test carrying a maximum of 200 marks in respect of only those candidates who are declared qualified on the results of Written Examination.

Part I Written Examination

Subject Maximum marks
Paper-I General Ability Test (English, General Knowledge and Psychological Test) 200
Paper-II Physical Sciences (Physics and Chemistry) 200
Paper-III Mathematics 200
Total marks 600

Part 2 Personality Test:

Those going on to the Personality Test will interviewed by a Board. Questions will span academic and extramural topics and matters of general interest. The objective of the Personality Test is to assess the candidate's initiative, leadership and other qualities.

Eligibility Overview:

Age Limit 17 to 21 years
Age Relaxation As per category
Educational Qualification The candidate must have passed in the first or second division in the higher secondary(12 years)examination under 10+2 pattern of school education with Mathematics and at least one of the subject Physics and Chemistry as subjects of the examination.
OR candidates must passed the 1st year examination under the 3 years degree course from the recognised university
Nationality Indian citizens only
